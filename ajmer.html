Welcome to Ajmer

Explore the sacred city of Ajmer, renowned for the historic Ajmer Sharif Dargah, beautiful lakes, and vibrant culture. A melting pot of spirituality, history, and commerce, Ajmer offers something special for every traveler.

Ajmer is one of the oldest cities of Rajasthan, famous for the shrine of Sufi saint Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti. The city has many attractions like Anasagar Lake, Adhai-din-ka-Jhonpra mosque, and the annual Urs festival that attracts devotees from across the world.

Ajmer also serves as a gateway to Pushkar, known for its sacred lake and Brahma temple. The city blends the historical with the modern, making it a vibrant place to visit and explore.
